🕐 Time Zone Information

Local Time: GMT (Greenwich Mean Time)

Time Difference: 6 hours ahead of Central Time

Jet Lag Tips: Start adjusting sleep schedule 3 days before departure. Stay hydrated during flight and get sunlight exposure upon arrival.

🌤️ Weather & Climate

Expected Weather: Mild and wet autumn weather

Temperature Range: 10-16°C (50-61°F) with frequent rain

Clothing Recommendations: Layers, waterproof jacket, comfortable walking shoes with good grip

🌍 Cultural Guidelines

Tipping: 10-15% in restaurants if service charge not included. Round up taxi fares. £1-2 per bag for hotel porters.
Pub Etiquette: Order at the bar, don't wait for table service. It's customary to buy rounds for your group.
Queueing: Always queue politely and wait your turn. Queue jumping is considered very rude.
Greetings: A simple 'hello' or 'good morning' is appropriate. Handshakes for introductions.

🗣️ Language Basics

Thank you Cheers (informal) / Thank you (formal)
Excuse me Sorry / Excuse me
Where is the bathroom? Where's the loo? / Where are the toilets?
How much does this cost? How much is this? / What's the price?

🍴 Dining Tips & Etiquette

Meal Times: Lunch is typically 12:00-2:00 PM, dinner 6:00-9:00 PM. Many kitchens close between services.
Payment: Most places accept cards, but some smaller pubs are cash only. Service charge may be included.
Reservations: Always book for dinner, especially weekends. Lunch reservations recommended for popular spots.
