FAQ

Can I self‑host with my own LLM keys?
Yes. Voygent is open to self‑host (BYOK). Our hosted plans simply add convenience, uptime, and features like custom domains and team dashboards.
Do you support CPMaxx, VAX, and Delta Vacations?
Yes — via local browser automation today (mcp‑chrome). We normalize results to a standard schema so AI can plan with live availability. Hosted automation is on our roadmap.
What about saving hotel photos?
We recommend saving them at publish time so proposals are fast and future‑proof even if URLs change.
Can I use cheaper models to draft and premium to polish?
Absolutely. Draft with budget models; apply a premium pass for final client‑ready copy.
